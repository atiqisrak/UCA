OUR HISTORY

United College of Aviation, Science & Management (UCA) is a sister concern of OS City Group, one of the biggest conglomerate in Bangladesh. It is a very fast growing world class University College for specialized Higher education, under National University (College Code: 6597), affiliated by Ministry of Education, Government of the Peoples Republic of Bangladesh. Our college is approved by a prominent UK Education Board, BTEC – EDEXCEL, having center number 93254 and Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB) (College code: 50416) and also ISO 9001:2008 certified. UCA is a member of OMAR-SULTAN Foundation (a non-political charitable organization which offers scholarship to poor and meritorious students across the country).

The OS City group since its establishment have been contributing to the educational institutes and students to create a Digital Bangladesh and skilled nation. This group extended its hand to top ranked Bangladeshi Universities for a better and quality education and also help the poor and economically dissolvent students of Bangladesh. ‘Omar Sultan Foundation Computer Lab’ at Dhaka University and ‘Omar Sultan Dental Department’ at USTC, Chittagong are 02 mention worthy recent donations of OS City Group. The group dreams to make this University College as a complete world class university soon.

The beautiful College Campus at its own land with international standard structure and design, 24 hrs electricity, central ac facilities, modern equipped labs, capsule lift, multimedia library, and Wi-Fi zone Campus is mention worthy.
