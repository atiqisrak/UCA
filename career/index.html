Female Cabin crew JOB:
——————————————————–
CV submission Last date: 10 February 2019
Send CV to jobs@uca.edu.bd or following address
———————————————————
Interview Date: 21st February 2019 – Thursday
Time: 9 am to 5 pm
Venue: United College of Aviation, Science & Management, Sector 3,Road-4,House-16 ,Uttara, Dhaka

Recruit Cabin Crew from Bangladesh for Air Arabia,
the airline based in Sharjah UAE
========================================
Candidate Eligibility Criteria:
• A minimum education up to Higher Secondary level
• Age between 20 years and 25 years.
• Fluency in English & Bengali language (Written and Spoken). Fluency in other languages will be an advantage.
• Must meet the height and weight criteria within BMI. (Female: Minimum Height -160 cm – Weight to be in proportion to Age and Height)
• Ability to swim without a flotation device.
• Clear skin with no marks / tattoos that would be visible whilst wearing the cabin crew uniform.
• Medically fit to meet regulatory requirements.
• Prior experience in Customer service, Hospitality, Cabin Crew, Nursing will be preferred but not essential.
==============================================
Candidate Requirement:
• Dynamic and excellent in customer service
• Good communication, assertive with positive attitude and body language
• Greeting passengers as they board and exit the plane and provide assistance during the flight
• Good in team work and creates friendly atmosphere on-board
• Manage In-flight Sales and cash handling
• Punctual to work as per schedule and deliver service at highest standards
——————————————————————————–
Required documents to be submitted by the candidates in order to be shortlisted to attend the Assessment day & hard copies of the following to be brought on the Assessment day:
• Completed Candidate Declaration form
• Completed Candidate Details form
• Updated CV
• 2 Passport size Photographs & 1 Full Length Photograph in Business Attire & 1 in Casual Attire
• Clear Passport copy
———————————————————————————-
• You may check this link for Cabin Crew position eligibility criteria: https://www.airarabia.com/en/careers
==============================================
• About Air Arabia:
Air Arabia (PJSC), listed on the Dubai Financial Market, is the Middle East and North Africa’s leading low-cost carrier (LCC). Air Arabia commenced operations in October 2003 and currently operates a total fleet of 53 new Airbus A320 aircraft, serving some 150 routes from four hubs in the UAE, Morocco, and Egypt. Air Arabia is an award-winning airline that focuses on offering comfort, reliability and value-for-money air travel.
