About Us

"United College of Aviation, Science and Management" aims to provide high quality learning opportunities to meet the needs of individuals, employers and the nation.

Student success is at the heart of our work….

We believe that enabling students to succeed is our core purpose and that all staff, whatever their role, make a vital contribution to a successful and rewarding learning experience for our students.

We have high expectations and aim for excellence….

We believe in supporting our students and staff to achieve their full potential and that high standards should be the norm. We expect to challenge, praise and inspire our students. We are not satisfied with merely adequate performance in any area of our work. We want students tell us how they learn best and how we can best meet their needs.

We respect everyone and celebrate diversity

We believe that every student matters and that trust and mutual respect are essential to the health of our organization. We believe in creating an environment where difference and diversity are respected.

We are committed to teamwork, sharing good practice and partnership

We recognize that where all staffs share common values we are successful. We encourage all staffs to innovate, create and contribute. We expect all our staffs work as ambassador for the College. We recognize the need to create opportunities for the free flow of ideas and contributions from all staff.

We aim to promote sustainability and use our resources efficiently

We support environmentally responsible practices in our use of resources and the development of the college buildings and estate. We believe that safe, secure, welcoming learning and working environments are the prerequisite for success.
